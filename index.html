iTransition Applicaton

Welcome to the iTransition App. This app is for students with disabilities with an IEP, ages 14 and older.

An IEP details your special education services. This app produces a “Draft Transition Plan,” based on your answers.

A “Transition Plan” is part of your IEP document. The plan is your road map to transition to adult life after high school.

I am:

Your IEP team is required to begin transition planning at age 14. You MUST be invited to IEP meetings when transition planning is discussed. Your post-secondary dreams dictate necessary transition planning. Your IEP team must develop a statement of required transition services to help you prepare you for adult life. For more information on transition planning see FAQ # _____.

Transition planning continues during your 15th year. Transition planning should become more focused around your interests after high school.

Your IEP team is required to have a transition goal developed by age 16. The transition goal must ensure you have the necessary courses to graduate from high school and prepare you for post-secondary academics, working and living. Your lifetime goals guide the transition goal. The District is required to make a referral to Kansas Vocational Rehabilitation at age 16. For more information on services available from Kansas Vocational Rehabilitation see FAQ #_________.

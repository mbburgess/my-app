The IDEA requires certain types of people attend IEP meetings, such as your parent(s), teachers, psychologists, school administrators, etc. You must be invited to attend IEP meetings when your team is discussing your transition plans. You also have the right to attend non-transition IEP meetings.

I speak and participate in my IEP meetings:

The IDEA requires your school send a written notice of IEP meetings to your parent (s) 10 days prior to the meeting. The Notice must specify the items to be discussed during the meeting. If you have an IEP, are 14 + years, you should be invited to attend IEP transition meetings. There should be at least one IEP transition meeting per year.
